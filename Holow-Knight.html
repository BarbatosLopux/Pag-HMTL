DESCEND INTO THE DARK

Explore the Depths of a Lost Realm

Underneath the vanishing town of Dirtmouth lies an expansive, age-old realm. Countless venture below the surface, seeking wealth, honor, or revelations of ancient enigmas. Embark as the mysterious Knight, journeying through the abyss, unveiling its enigmas, and triumphing over its malevolence.





The history of Hallownest

Before the realm of Hallownest, there existed an all-powerful creature, The Radiance, which through its light gave birth to the tribe of Moths. Together, they created the kingdom of Hallownest, a place for insects to live out their lives. Insects of all kinds soon arrived. The Mantis tribe, a warrior people, and the Bee tribe, a hierarchical society, were among the many species that came to the new realm. While they respected The Radiance as the ultimate authority, the Mantis had their own government, and the bees secluded themselves in their hive, oblivious to all that transpired in Hallownest. All was peace and harmony, or so it seemed. In reality, The Radiance was a parasite that exploited the weak minds of the insects who worshipped her, for her own ends. In return, she granted them the wishes they sought. This was already significant, even if the insects remained unaware, and those who did realize it often didn't care. Their possessed minds weren't a problem compared to the wishes they obtained. No one cared until a being arrived that would change everything.





The infection

The Radiance had been plotting her revenge all this time, and she succeeded. She once again exploited the minds of weak insects, who, even if they forgot her, remembered the pleasure they felt when receiving wishes. She appeared to them in dreams, and over time, the dreams became more frequent and painful. It was like an infection. Eventually, the poor insects couldn't take it anymore and went mad, becoming aggressive and stronger. The dream was so potent that it took on a physical form, like a mucus or orange liquid (visible in Infected Crossroads), and spread through the air. The more privileged tribes and insects with stronger minds managed to survive, although some couldn't resist the temptation of possessing such power. The Radiance was no longer content with ruling minds; now, she wanted to destroy everything. It seemed all was lost. The King attempted to find a solution to this plague, believing that to overcome the light, darkness was required. Thus, he ventured into the Abyss and began experimenting with it to forge a pure vessel. He needed to create something capable of containing the infection within, and as the Void was emptiness, he harnessed it to attempt to forge a being fit for this purpose. His initial experiments didn't yield favorable results, nor did his subsequent ones. It took many years for him to discover a pure vessel, while the Radiance threatened to annihilate the insects. Until one day, he succeeded.





The Hollow Knight

The Hollow Knight He created the perfect vessel, or so he believed. It was the Hollow Knight, or the Empty Knight. The King needed a being incapable of feeling, and this one appeared to fit the bill. The Hollow Knight absorbed the Radiance, and she became trapped in its mind, like in a dream. To ensure security, the King enlisted three beings of great power to become the seals that would contain the Hollow Knight, in case something went awry. They were Monomon, the Teacher, who held vast wisdom and studied extensively in the archives. There was also Lurien, the Watcher, who kept watch over the City of Tears from his Tower and, out of loyalty to his king, accepted this sacrifice. Lastly, Herrah, the Beast, queen of the creatures of Deepnest, initially unwilling to sacrifice herself, but after striking a deal with the king, she acquiesced. The three became the Dreamers, sleeping eternally, and from their dreams, they kept the Hollow Knight in check. Time passed, and the King died, the Pale Lady hid away, and the group of the Five Knights disbanded. And even though everything was supposed to be under control, there was something that kept people uneasy. As mentioned before, the Hollow Knight was not perfect. It did possess a feeling, loyalty toward the king. The king treated the Hollow Knight as if it were his child, and it developed an attachment to the king. This showed that the Empty Knight wasn't so empty after all. The Radiance seized upon this and spread once more. Not even the Dreamers could stand against her, but they couldn't awaken because they now had to keep the Hollow Knight imprisoned. The Hollow Knight's void was filled with infection, and now she controlled its body, using it as a conduit. If the Hollow Knight were to escape, the infection would spread even faster. The weak insects became infected once again. The kingdom fell back into ruin, and without their king, their hopes dwindled. And at this point is when our character arrives in Hallownest, another vessel that must carve a path through this devastated realm to either replace the Hollow Knight as a pure vessel or put an end to the Radiance once and for all.

CHARACTERES

The Knight

The character that the player controls throughout the main story in Hollow Knight. At the start of the game, the Knight is equipped with only their Old Nail and the ability to heal themselves using SOUL. Throughout the game, they acquire new abilities and items that enable them to progress within Hallownest, the kingdom in which the game takes place, unlocking new areas and characters.

Hornet

Skillful guardian of the ruins of Hallownest. Wields a needle and thread.

The radiance

The light, long forgotten The plague, the infection, the madness that reanimates the corpses of Hallownest... the radiance that emanates from the eyes of a dead kingdom. Whence does it come? I suppose it's something beyond the understanding of mortals like me.

The Pale King

The Pale King was the monarch of Hallownest, the companion of the Pale Lady, and the ruler of the White Palace. When Hallownest eventually fell into ruin, its solitary king left his now-vanished palace and his subjects behind, retreating into hiding.

The five great Knights

Five legendary figures who once held the role of defending the kingdom. Their names have been forgotten by history. The five Great Knights are:


  • Fierce Dryya
  • Kindhearted Isma
  • Mysterious Ze'mer
  • Loyal Ogrim
  • Mighty Hegemol

MAP

In Hollow Knight, a valuable ally is undoubtedly a comprehensive map, as the level design showcases a labyrinthine nature and the extensive world layout can easily lead us astray in the depths of Hallownest. Navigating through these unfamiliar terrains can sow uncertainty regarding our goals and destinations. As we progress in our adventure, we will come across a variety of challenging bosses, collectible items of all sorts, intriguing missions, picturesque shops, needy rescued grubs, ornamental trinkets, hidden rarities, empowering abilities, magical spells, as well as strategically placed fast travel points, among many other things. This wealth of elements can result in us inadvertently overlooking certain aspects, prompting us to invest hours meticulously exploring the map in search of the final details necessary to achieve the coveted 107% completion.

ABOUT TEAM CHERRY



Team Cherry is a team of 3 people who have been involved in the development of Hollow Knight.



Ari Gibson

Ari is one of the Co-directors of Team Cherry. Previously, he ran the animation studio Mechanical Apple and has worked on countless projects in the fields of film, video games, and music videos. Ari has been modding games since before computers had color and has loved them since his first ever game rental: Faxanadu for NES.

William pellen

William is one of the Co-directors of Team Cherry. With years of experience in game design and development, he has been crafting imaginative worlds for players to explore and interact with. His passion for games ignited when his Dad discovered the wing boots for him in Zelda 2, and ever since, he has been driven to recreate that sense of adventure and excitement for others.

Jack Vine

Jack is the incredibly skilled coder of Team Cherry. With expertise in building and refining systems, optimizing performance, and introducing a range of thrilling new features, Jack has been an integral part of Team Cherry for quite some time. Their contributions were instrumental in bringing Hollow Knight to consoles and in the development of its expansive final free Content Pack, Godmaster.
